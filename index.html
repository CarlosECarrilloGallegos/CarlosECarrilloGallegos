Carlos Carrillo-Gallegos

Research Support Associate @ MIT Fiore Group

Hey, I'm Carlos! I currently work in Professor Arlene Fiore's group in MIT's Department of Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences. My research focuses on implementing a Bayesian Nonparametric ensembling method to create more accurate estimates of air pollutant concentrations and their uncertainties. These more accurate estimates will then be used in studies to investiage the correlation between air pollution and health outcomes. Moving forward, I'd like to continue working on the intersection of public health and air pollution, with a particular interest on using satellite derived data to drive this research. Before joining the lab, I received a Bachelor of Science from Yale University, studying Astrophysics.
